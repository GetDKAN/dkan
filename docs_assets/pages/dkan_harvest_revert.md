@page dkanharvestrevert dkan:harvest:revert

Revert a harvest, i.e. unpublish all of its harvested entities.

#### Arguments

- **id**. The source to revert.

#### Example

- <code>dkan:harvest:revert id</code>

#### Aliases

- dkan-harvest:revert
